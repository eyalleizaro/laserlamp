# Safety Notes

This project involves laser or high-brightness LED modules. Safety should be considered during design, testing, and use.

## General Safety

- Never look directly into a laser beam.
- Never point lasers toward people, animals, vehicles, windows, mirrors, or reflective surfaces.
- Test the electronics with regular LEDs before connecting laser modules.
- Disconnect power before changing wiring.
- Use low-power modules only.
- Use an enclosure, diffuser, or cover when possible.
- Clearly label the lamp as a laser/light device.

## Electrical Safety

- Check the voltage and current requirements of every module.
- Do not power the circuit from an unknown or unstable power supply.
- Avoid exposed wires.
- Do not touch the circuit while it is powered.
